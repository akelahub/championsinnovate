The Search Methodology

These candidates were sourced and selected by Akela Hub using the Akela Nexus proprietary methodology.

By leveraging advanced AI-driven market scanning combined with expert human curation, Akela Nexus identifies high-potential startups that align with specific social impact goals. The selection process evaluates companies on their ability to provide scalable, digital, and engaging solutions that address the specific pain points of Parents, Coaches, the Workforce, and Women in sport.
